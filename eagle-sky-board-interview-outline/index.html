Welcome

Dear Eagle Sky board,

My team and I have the privilege of helping capture the Eagle Sky story. Boe Parrish has been a mentor of mine for years, and after hearing so much about what God is doing through this camp, I'm thrilled to experience it first hand!

We will be conducting individual interviews with each board member during the May meeting (couples will be interviewed together). The purpose of these conversations is to capture the story of Eagle Sky Camp through your eyes — your experiences, your memories, and the lessons you've learned along the way. The best preparation is simply to spend a little time reflecting on the questions below before we sit down together.

We look forward to meeting everyone soon!

Holden Hill
Founder, Storyline Creative
What to Expect Each session is about an hour and a half. We'll have a video set staged at camp where you'll be the main subject on camera, and I'll sit just off to the side guiding you through a relaxed conversation.
Interview Questions
Please review these questions before your interview
01
Your Story
How did you come to know Jesus in your life, and how did He first connect you to Eagle Sky?
02
Camp Roots
Did you go to a summer camp when you were a kid? What kind of impact did it have on you?
03
First Experience
What's one of the first experiences you had at Eagle Sky that made you realize this place was special?
04
The Challenge Before Us
What's the greatest challenge you see kids facing today?
05
All In
A lot of people see youth camp ministry as secondary. Why was Eagle Sky something you were willing to go all in on?
06
The Vision
How would you describe Eagle Sky's vision in your own words?
07
Changed Lives
What's a story you've seen personally of how Eagle Sky has changed a camper's life?
08
Through a Camper's Eyes
If you got to be a kid again and go to Eagle Sky for a week, what would you be most excited about? (Think fun here!)
09
Turning Point
If you had to pick one week, one summer, or one event that shaped the trajectory of Eagle Sky in a powerful way, what would it be?
10
The Next Generation
What do you see in the next generation, and how do you believe God is going to use them to change the world?
11
Biggest Lesson
What is the biggest lesson God has taught you personally through your time at Eagle Sky?
12
Pass the Torch
How do you want to encourage and challenge the next generation of Eagle Sky leaders?
A note on preparation: You don't need to write out answers ahead of time. But if specific people, moments, or stories come to mind as you read through these questions, jot them down. The most valuable material comes from real memories and real names — not polished summaries.
Thank You
